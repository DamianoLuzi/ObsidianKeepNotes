- [ ] 1. "hey, quick question... how's your day going so far?" (simple. human. works anywhere.)
- [ ] 2. "i'm curious... have you been here before?" (great for gyms, classes, events, cafés.)
- [ ] 3. "what are you working on right now?" (opens people up fast without being weird.)
- [ ] 4. "i always see you around, what's your schedule like?" (instantly smooth in any recurring environment.)
- [ ] 5. "you look like you know this place... what should i try?" (food, gym equipment, campus, workspace)
- [ ] 6. "what brings you here today?" (non-threatening, flexible, always answers.)
- [ ] 7. "do you come here at this time usually?" (invites routine-based conversation.)
- [ ] 8. "how long have you been doing this?" (great for gym, hobbies, events.)
- [ ] 9. "is it usually this busy?" (shared observation. low effort. works everywhere.)
- [ ] 10. "i noticed your `_____`. where'd you get it?" (jacket, shoes, backpack, bottle, whatever. easy win.)
- [ ] 11. "what do you do when you're not here?" (open-ended without being heavy.)
- [ ] 12. "hey, i'm trying to figure this out... do you know how it works?" (equipment, process, event, line, system.)
- [ ] 13. "are you from around here?"
- [ ] 14. "i've been meaning to ask... what's your name?" (when you've seen them more than once.)
- [ ] 15. "did you see what just happened?" (the universal cheat code: comment on the environment.)
