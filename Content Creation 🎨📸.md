## 💡 The Bottom Line / Introspection

> So what’s the bottom line? For the past like 8 bloody months I have been nothing but day dreaming about a better future I always knew I was meant for more more but I kept escaping always caught up in distractions. Everyday I put on a mask, molded myself into someone that would just fit into society and no one would notice or question the integrity of. 
> 
> In the meantime I kept asking myself who am I; my real identity kept getting more and more buried into limiting beliefs and stories I told myself which kept me stuck. That little kid that used to play with Legos, climb trees, admire the beauty of nature—that curious, playful, annoying, at the time pretty fat kid—wouldn’t have wanted me to suffer this way. 
> 
> On the other hand, by stressing myself out with all this negativity I was hurting both myself and him, not achieving the dreams we always envisioned together. I think now times finally over. The time has come to take action, to make a bold move, and get a hold of my life and the future I want.
